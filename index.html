How it works

Finding a tutor is easy. Once you fill out the Find a tutor form, we'll send your information to every tutor on our platform and tutors will reach out to you directly. Once you find a tutor, you'll work with them to schedule sessions and you'll pay them directly. Tutors accept Cash or Venmo.

  1. 1 Submit the "Find a tutor" form.
  2. 2 We'll send your request to every tutor on our platform.
  3. 3 Available tutors will reach out to you directly.
  4. 4 Schedule sessions and pay your tutor directly.
